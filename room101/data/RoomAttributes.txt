# This file is for adding room attributes on the form
# 'addroomattribute $ <name of attribute> $ <description of the attribute>'
# For example:
# addRoomAttribute $ Seaview $ You can see the sea from here
# -----------------------------------------------------------

addRoomAttribute $ Seaview $ You can see the sea from here
addRoomAttribute $ Television $ The room has a TV
addRoomAttribute $ Storage-safe $ The room has a secure safe to store valuables
addRoomAttribute $ Bed $ The room has a bed
